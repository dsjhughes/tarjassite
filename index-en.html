About

Studio Eveliina creates and makes reality of your clothing dreams. I have been designing and making clothing for the past 30 years and I have extensive knowledge of the garment making industry. Together we sketch first on paper your ideas and thoughts. Possibly with the help of some pictures you may have gathered together of what you have in mind.

Services

The fabric is to be provided by the customer and if necessary I can help you choose the right one. I would also be very happy to resurrect and bring back to life some old and much loved outfits you might have, we can discuss which would be the best option for you. I do alterations too. Because new ready made outfits can easily be altered to look like made to measure. So that you leave looking and feeling a million dollars. Opening hours can be flexible on appointment only, please call in advance to arrange a time. Guide prices are available in the shop or call me for a quote.
